CARAMEL MACCHIATO WITH VANILLA SYRUP

(181)

Caramel Macchiato With Vanilla Syrup

PREP TIME

5 minutes

COOK TIME

5 minutes

LEVEL

Easy

Lightly sweetened vanilla milk mixed with espresso and topped with buttery caramel sauce.

INGREDIENTS

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Prepare the espresso using a macchinetta or espresso machine.
  2. While the espresso is being made, warm up the milk and froth it.
  3. Pour the espresso into a measuring cup.
  4. Add vanilla and frothed milk to the cup. Then, pour the espresso on top.
  5. Drizzle the caramel on top.
